About South County Child & Family Consultants

Since our founding, we have been dedicated to offering comprehensive psychological evaluations tailored to the unique needs of children, adolescents, and their families in South County and surrounding areas.

Our Mission & Approach

Our mission is to provide high-quality, evidence-based psychological services in a supportive and confidential setting. We believe in a collaborative approach, working closely with families, schools, and other professionals to ensure the best possible outcomes.

  • Individualized assessment plans.
  • Experienced and compassionate clinicians.
  • Focus on strengths and actionable recommendations.
  • Commitment to confidentiality and ethical practice.
